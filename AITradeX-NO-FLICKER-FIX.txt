AITradeX No Flicker / No Blink Fix

Base:
- AITradeX-Final-NewUserTracking.zip

Reason:
- Mobile/low-power browsers can show screen light blinking due to repeated repaint from glow, shimmer, background effects, chart iframe reload/repaint, and decorative animations.

Fix:
- Disabled non-essential animations.
- Reduced heavy glow/shadow.
- Stabilized chart/iframe repaint background.
- Disabled decorative flashing/shimmer effects.
- Added reduced-motion safety CSS.

No logic changes.
No SQL required.
Admin/user functionality unchanged.
